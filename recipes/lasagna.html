Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian dish features layers of savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets. Topped with a generous helping of cheese, it is baked until golden brown and irresistibly bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Onion, garlic, carrot, and celery
  • Crushed tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Dried herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh bazil

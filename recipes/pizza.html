Pizza

Description

Pizza is a globally beloved dish originating from Naples, Italy, consisting of a flattened dough base topped with tomato sauce and cheese. It is traditionally baked at high temperatures in a wood-fired oven to achieve a charred, crispy crust and bubbly toppings. From the simplicity of a classic Margherita to the endless variety of modern toppings, pizza remains one of the most versatile and popular comfort foods in the world.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose or bread flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Dried oregano or basil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, salt, and oil.
  2. Let the dough rise in a warm spot until it doubles in size.
  3. Stretch the dough into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce and add mozzarella and toppings.
  5. Bake at a high temperature until the crust is golden and the cheese melts.
